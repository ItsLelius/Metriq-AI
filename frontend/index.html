FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about Metriq.

What is Metriq?
+

Metriq is an AI-powered education analytics platform that mines and analyzes online learning behavior data to identify student clusters, predict dropout risk, and surface insights that drive student success.

Where does the data come from?
+

The base dataset is sourced from Kaggle — a public online learning dataset with 5,000+ records. Additional data can be contributed through the CSV upload feature or the live survey form.

What does the Data Pipeline page show?
+

It walks through all 17 steps of the data mining process — from loading the raw dataset to saving the cleaned output. Each step is interactive with before and after comparisons and plain English explanations.

What are the 3 student clusters?
+

After applying PCA and K-Means clustering, students are grouped into three behavioral clusters — High Performers, Passive Learners, and At-Risk — based on their engagement, completion, and activity patterns.

Who is Metis?
+

Metis is the AI chatbot built into Metriq, powered by the Gemini API. Named after the Greek goddess of wisdom, Metis answers questions about the data, explains pipeline steps, and helps interpret cluster results.
